Summary: Latitude vs. X

The Purpose of this project was to analyze how weather chagnes as you get closer to the equater. As we began our project we had to decide where and how we were going to get our data. We decided to use a Weather API to create a dataset of over 500 cities.

After we got the data we used various ploting methods using MatPlotlib to retrive different data that we thought would be helpful in proving our hypothisis. The 4 factors we decided to base our story on include temperature, cloudiness, wind speeds, and the humidity in the 500 different cities. This site will be able to show you the dataset we used as well as the results of our analysis as trends were witnessed.
